<p><p>The mechanical ventilation system market can be categorized into three primary types: ceiling-mount, wall-mount, and cabinet-mount systems. Ceiling-mount systems are installed in ceilings to optimize space and ensure even air distribution, making them ideal for large areas. Wall-mount systems are versatile and easy to install, fitting into smaller spaces efficiently while providing adequate air circulation. Cabinet-mount systems are typically compact and designed for enclosed spaces, offering controlled ventilation without taking up significant room, suitable for residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
