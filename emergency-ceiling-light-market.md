<p><p>The emergency ceiling light market can be categorized into three main types based on their power systems. Self-contained power systems have integrated batteries that allow the light to operate independently during power outages. Central power systems rely on a centralized battery bank that powers multiple lights, offering a collective solution for larger spaces. Hybrid power systems combine features of both, utilizing both integrated batteries and centralized power sources, ensuring enhanced reliability and efficiency in various emergency scenarios.</p></p>
