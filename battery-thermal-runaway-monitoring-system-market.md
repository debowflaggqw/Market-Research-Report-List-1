<p><p>The Battery Thermal Runaway Monitoring System market includes two primary types: Wired Monitoring Systems and Wireless Monitoring Systems. Wired Monitoring Systems utilize physical connections to continuously monitor battery temperatures and detect potential thermal runaway events. These systems often offer reliable performance and lower latency. In contrast, Wireless Monitoring Systems provide flexibility and ease of installation, transmitting data via wireless signals, which enhances mobility and scalability. Both systems are crucial for ensuring battery safety and preventing catastrophic failures in various applications.</p></p>
