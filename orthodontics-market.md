<p><p>The orthodontics market comprises several key segments: brackets, anchorage appliances, and archwires. Brackets are the components bonded to teeth to hold archwires in place, facilitating tooth movement. Anchorage appliances provide stability and support during treatment, ensuring proper alignment and preventing unwanted tooth movement. Archwires are the metal wires that connect brackets and exert force on teeth to guide them into the desired position. Together, these elements are essential for effective orthodontic treatment and improving dental health.</p></p>
