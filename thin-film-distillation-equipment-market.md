<p><p>The thin film distillation equipment market is primarily divided into two types: horizontal and vertical thin film evaporators. Horizontal thin film evaporators utilize a horizontal configuration to enable efficient liquid film formation and evaporation, ideal for processing viscous materials. In contrast, vertical thin film evaporators operate on a vertical axis, enhancing contact time and improving heat transfer, making them suitable for various applications in the chemical, pharmaceutical, and food industries. Both types optimize evaporation and concentration processes.</p></p>
