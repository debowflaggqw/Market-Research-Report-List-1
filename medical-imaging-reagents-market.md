<p><p>The medical imaging reagents market comprises three main types: contrast reagents, optical reagents, and nuclear reagents. Contrast reagents enhance the visibility of structures in imaging techniques like X-ray and MRI, improving diagnosis accuracy. Optical reagents are used in techniques such as fluorescence imaging, allowing real-time observation of biological processes. Nuclear reagents, including radiopharmaceuticals, are employed in nuclear medicine for imaging and therapy, facilitating the visualization of metabolic activity and aiding in the detection of diseases.</p></p>
