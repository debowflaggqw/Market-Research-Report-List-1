<p><p>The benchtop parts washer market can be categorized into solvent-based and water/aqueous-based types. Solvent-based washers use organic solvents for effective cleaning of grease and oils, offering strong degreasing capabilities but may pose health and environmental risks. In contrast, water/aqueous-based washers utilize biodegradable detergents combined with water, making them eco-friendly and safer for operators. They effectively remove contaminants while minimizing hazardous waste, catering to industries emphasizing environmental compliance and worker safety. Each type serves different cleaning needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
