<p><strong>The Hemostatic Pad Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gelatin Hemostatic Pad</li><li>Fibrin Hemostatic Pad</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemostatic pad market consists of two main types: gelatin hemostatic pads and fibrin hemostatic pads. Gelatin hemostatic pads utilize porcine or bovine gelatin to promote clot formation, providing rapid hemostatic action and biocompatibility for various surgical procedures. Fibrin hemostatic pads, on the other hand, are derived from human plasma and enhance natural coagulation processes, aiding in complex surgeries and trauma care. Both types are crucial for effectively controlling bleeding, thereby improving patient outcomes in medical settings.</p></p>
