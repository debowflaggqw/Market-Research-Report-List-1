<p><p>Composite bearings are advanced components made from a combination of materials to enhance performance and durability. The Fiber Matrix market utilizes reinforced fibers, such as carbon or glass, embedded in a polymer matrix, providing high strength-to-weight ratios and improved wear resistance. Conversely, the Metal Matrix market combines metals with ceramic or polymer materials, offering excellent thermal stability and load-bearing capacities. Both types cater to various applications in aerospace, automotive, and industrial sectors, delivering superior performance over traditional bearing materials.</p></p>
