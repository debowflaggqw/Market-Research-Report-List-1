<p><p>The PLC (Planar Lightwave Circuit) fiber splitter market encompasses various configurations, including 1x4, 1x8, and 1x16 splitters, which denote the number of output fibers from a single input. A 1x4 splitter divides the input signal into four outputs, while a 1x8 splitter branches it into eight, and a 1x16 splitter provides sixteen outputs. Additional types, categorized as "Others," may include higher split ratios or specialized models for specific applications. These devices are essential for efficient fiber-optic network distribution.</p></p>
