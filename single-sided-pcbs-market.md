<p><p>The single-sided PCB market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Single-sided Rigid PCB** - These are made from rigid materials and feature components on one side, commonly used in consumer electronics due to their stability and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>2. **Single-sided Flexible PCB** - These PCBs utilize flexible materials, allowing them to bend and fit into compact spaces, ideal for wearable devices and mobile applications.</p><p>3. **Single-sided Rigid-flex PCB** - A hybrid of rigid and flexible technologies, these PCBs offer design versatility and are used in complex applications requiring both rigidity and flexibility.</p></p>
