<p><p>Impact mills are essential devices used in various industrial processes to crush and grind materials through high-velocity impacts. The market for impact mills is segmented into two main types: horizontal and vertical impact mills. Horizontal impact mills utilize a horizontal rotor design for efficient grinding, suitable for coarse to medium particle sizes. In contrast, vertical impact mills feature a vertical rotor, optimized for fine grinding and precision particle size control, making them ideal for applications requiring finer materials.</p></p>
