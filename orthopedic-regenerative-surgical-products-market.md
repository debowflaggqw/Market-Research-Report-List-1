<p><p>The Orthopedic Regenerative Surgical Products market encompasses several types of offerings. Allografts are biological tissues sourced from donors, used to repair or replace damaged tissue. Synthetic products, often made from materials like polymers or ceramics, mimic natural bone structure. Cell-based products involve the use of stem cells or other cell types to promote regeneration. Viscosupplements are hyaluronic acid injections that enhance joint lubrication, reducing pain and improving mobility in osteoarthritis patients. Each type addresses specific orthopedic needs.</p></p>
