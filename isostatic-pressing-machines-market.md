<p><p>Isostatic pressing machines are used to achieve uniform compaction in materials through the application of pressure. The market for these machines is divided into two main types: cold isostatic pressing machines, which utilize room temperature pressure to compact materials without altering their properties, and hot isostatic pressing machines, which apply heat along with pressure to enhance material density and improve mechanical properties. Both types serve various industries, including ceramics, metals, and composites, optimizing production processes and material performance.</p></p>
