<p><strong>The Snow Melting Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydronic</li><li>Electric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Snow melting systems primarily fall into two categories: hydronic and electric. Hydronic systems circulate heated water through pipes installed beneath surfaces. They efficiently melt snow and ice, making them ideal for larger areas but requiring a boiler and maintenance. Conversely, electric systems use embedded heating cables to generate heat directly, providing easier installation and control for smaller spaces. Both systems enhance safety and accessibility during winter, ensuring clear pathways and reducing labor for snow removal.</p></p>
