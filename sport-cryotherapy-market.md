<p><p>The sport cryotherapy market is primarily divided into two types: whole body cryotherapy and local cryotherapy. Whole body cryotherapy involves exposure to extremely low temperatures in a specialized chamber, promoting overall recovery, muscle relief, and inflammation reduction. In contrast, local cryotherapy targets specific body areas using localized cooling techniques, helping to alleviate pain, expedite healing, and reduce swelling in particular muscles or joints. Both methods are widely utilized by athletes to enhance performance and expedite recovery.</p></p>
