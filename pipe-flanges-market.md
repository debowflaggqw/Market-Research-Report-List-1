<p><strong>The Pipe Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blind flanges</li><li>lap joint</li><li>Slip-on flanges</li><li>Socket weld</li><li>Threaded flanges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipe flanges market encompasses various types essential for piping systems. Blind flanges seal off pipe ends, while lap joint flanges facilitate easy alignment and disassembly. Slip-on flanges slide over the pipe, allowing for quick installation, while socket weld flanges provide a strong connection for smaller pipes. Threaded flanges have internal threads, enabling easy attachment without welding. Each flange type serves unique applications in industries such as oil and gas, chemicals, and construction, contributing to the overall efficiency and safety of pipelines.</p></p>
