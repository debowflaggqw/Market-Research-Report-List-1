<p><ul><li>Intensity Modulated Fiber Optic Sensors</li><li>Phase Modulated Fiber Optic Sensors</li><li>Wavelength Modulated Fiber Optic Sensors</li><li>Polarization Modulated Fiber Optic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic displacement sensors utilize different modulation techniques to measure displacement with high precision. Intensity modulated sensors detect changes in light intensity to gauge distance. Phase modulated sensors analyze shifts in the phase of light waves, providing accurate measurements. Wavelength modulated sensors focus on variations in light wavelength, enabling precise displacement tracking. Polarization modulated sensors assess changes in the polarization state of light, offering unique advantages in specific applications. Together, these types enhance performance across various engineering and industrial sectors.</p></p>
