<p><p>The Venturi tubes market is categorized into two main types: built-in Venturi tubes and plug-in Venturi tubes. Built-in Venturi tubes are integrated into the piping system, offering a permanent and space-efficient solution for fluid flow measurement. They are commonly used in applications where consistent monitoring is required. In contrast, plug-in Venturi tubes are designed for easy installation and removal, making them suitable for temporary setups or applications requiring flexibility. Both types optimize flow measurement and enhance efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
