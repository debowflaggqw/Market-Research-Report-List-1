<p><p>The Emergency Location Transmitter market includes devices designed for locating individuals in distress. Emergency Location Transmitters provide essential tracking capabilities for emergency responders. Personal Locator Beacons (PLBs) are handheld devices that users activate to signal distress, transmitting location data via satellite systems. Emergency Position Indicating Radio Beacons (EPIRBs) are similar but primarily used in maritime contexts, automatically activating when they hit water to broadcast a distress signal. Together, these technologies enhance safety and response during emergencies.</p></p>
