<p><p>The fiber laser drilling machine for the automotive market utilizes two main types of lasers: pulsed fiber lasers and continuous wave (CW) fiber lasers. Pulsed fiber lasers deliver short bursts of high energy, enabling precise and controlled drilling of materials like metals and composites, ideal for intricate designs. In contrast, CW fiber lasers provide a constant beam of light, suitable for high-speed drilling and deeper penetration in thicker materials, enhancing productivity in automotive manufacturing and assembly processes.</p></p>
