<p><strong>The Cosmetic Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ablative</li><li>Non-Ablative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cosmetic laser market is primarily divided into two types: ablative and non-ablative lasers. Ablative lasers remove the outer layers of skin, promoting rapid healing and significant skin resurfacing, making them effective for deep wrinkles, scars, and pigmentation issues. In contrast, non-ablative lasers target deeper skin layers without damaging the surface, resulting in minimal downtime. They are suited for improving skin tone, texture, and collagen production, making them popular for milder skin rejuvenation treatments. Both types offer unique benefits based on individual needs.</p></p>
