<p><p>Filtration in the chemical processing market encompasses various types aimed at separating impurities from fluids. Liquid filtration focuses on removing suspended solids, microbes, and contaminants from liquids used in manufacturing, ensuring product purity and process efficiency. Gas filtration, on the other hand, targets airborne contaminants, particulates, and volatile organic compounds to protect equipment and maintain air quality in processing environments. Air filtration ensures compliance with environmental standards and enhances workplace safety, minimizing harmful emissions and improving overall operational efficacy.</p></p>
