<p><p>The laser communication market is categorized into three main types: lasers, modulators, and others. Lasers are the core components, responsible for generating coherent light signals that carry data over long distances. Modulators play a crucial role in encoding information onto these light signals, enabling efficient data transmission. The "others" segment includes additional components and technologies that support laser communication, such as receivers, amplifiers, and optical fibers. Together, these elements drive advancements in high-speed communication networks and technologies.</p></p>
