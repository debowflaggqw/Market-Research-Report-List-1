<p><p>Sliding speed gates can be classified into two market types: identifying and non-identifying. Identifying gates use biometric or card-based systems to recognize individuals, allowing for controlled access and enhanced security. They are commonly employed in high-security areas. On the other hand, non-identifying gates operate without personal identification, managing crowd flow and access without tracking individual users. These gates are often used in public places where security is important but personal identification is not required, such as airports or subway stations.</p></p>
