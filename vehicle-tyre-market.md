<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Vehicle Tyre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tyre</li><li>Bias Tyre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle tyre market primarily comprises radial and bias tyres, each serving distinct performance needs. Radial tyres feature layers of fabric arranged radially from the centre, allowing for improved fuel efficiency, better traction, and enhanced longevity. They are ideal for highway driving. In contrast, bias tyres have layers that crisscross, offering greater sidewall strength and resilience, making them suitable for off-road and heavy-duty applications. The choice between these types depends on vehicle usage, terrain, and driving conditions.</p></p>
