<p><p>Ethylene Propylene Rubber (EPR) encompasses two main types: Ethylene Propylene Monomer (EPM) and Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM). EPM is a saturated rubber that offers excellent aging resistance, flexibility, and good ozone resistance, making it ideal for applications like adhesives and electrical insulation. EPDM is a versatile diene rubber that adds unsaturation, enhancing compatibility with oils and improving weather resistance. Both materials find extensive use in automotive, construction, and industrial sectors, leveraging their unique properties for diverse applications.</p></p>
