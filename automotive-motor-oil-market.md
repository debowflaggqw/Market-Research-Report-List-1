<p><ul><li>Conventional Oil</li><li>Full-synthetic Oil</li><li>Synthetic-blend Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive motor oil market is segmented into three main types: conventional oil, full-synthetic oil, and synthetic-blend oil. Conventional oil is derived from crude oil, providing basic engine protection but requiring more frequent changes. Full-synthetic oil is chemically engineered for superior performance, offering better lubrication and engine longevity, suitable for high-performance vehicles. Synthetic-blend oil combines both conventional and synthetic oils, delivering enhanced protection and performance while being more affordable, making it a popular choice for everyday drivers.</p></p>
