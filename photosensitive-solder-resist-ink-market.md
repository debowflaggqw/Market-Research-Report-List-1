<p><p>The photosensitive solder resist ink market is divided into two main types: solvent-based and non-solvent-based inks. Solvent-based inks utilize organic solvents to dissolve the resin, offering flexibility and ease of application but can pose environmental concerns due to volatile organic compounds. Non-solvent-based inks, typically water-based or UV-curable, are more environmentally friendly, providing reduced emissions and improved safety. Both types are crucial for printed circuit board fabrication, enabling precise patterning and protection against solder during assembly processes.</p></p>
