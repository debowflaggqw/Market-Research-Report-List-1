<p><strong>The Medical Device Packaging Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monolayer Film</li><li>Coextruded Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical device packaging films market is primarily categorized into monolayer films and coextruded films. Monolayer films consist of a single layer, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, ideal for low-sensitivity applications. In contrast, coextruded films combine multiple layers, enhancing barrier properties and flexibility, making them suitable for more sensitive medical devices that require superior protection against contaminants and moisture. Both types play critical roles in ensuring the safety and integrity of medical products during storage and transportation.</p></p>
