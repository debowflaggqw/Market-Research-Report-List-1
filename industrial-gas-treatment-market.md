<p><p>The Industrial Gas Treatment market is primarily categorized into two types: Amines and Non-Amines. Amines, such as monoethanolamine and diethanolamine, are widely used for gas purification, particularly for CO2 removal in natural gas and flue gas applications due to their effectiveness and strong absorption properties. Non-Amines include solvent-based treatments like physical solvents and absorbents, often preferred for specific applications requiring lower volatility and reduced environmental impact. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing gas quality and meeting regulatory standards.</p></p>
